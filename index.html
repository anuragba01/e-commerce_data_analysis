Key Findings (Dataset 1)

Finding 1 · Discount Erosion

40%+ Discounts Destroy Profit

At a 40% discount, net profit collapses to below $0 (-$1.09). At 50%, each transaction loses an average of –$62.04. The safe ceiling is 30%.

Finding 2 · Regression Model

Quantity Drives Profit Most

OLS regression (R²=0.43) shows each additional unit in a transaction adds +$52.28 net profit. A 10% discount reduction adds back +$66.48.

Finding 3 · Category Leader

Electronics = 64% of Revenue

Electronics generates $3.38M of the platform's $5.28M total revenue. Clothing has the highest margin at 24.8%, making it the most profitable per-dollar category.

Finding 4 · Predictive Model

Gradient Boosting: 87% Accuracy

The Gradient Boosting model achieves an 87% accuracy predicting whether a transaction will land in a high-margin (>25%) bracket based on price, quantity, and discount.

Finding 5 · Price Correlation

Price Beats Volume for Revenue

Unit Price (r=+0.739) has a far stronger correlation to Revenue than Quantity (r=+0.333), demonstrating that pricing power outperforms aggressive sales volume tactics.

Finding 6 · Overall Margin

Average Net Margin: 23.65%

Across 10,000 transactions, the platform maintains an average margin of 23.65%, translating to $1.43M net profit on $5.28M total revenue.

Strategic Recommendations (Dataset 1)

Empirical Action Items & Policies

1
Cap Promotions at 25% Maximum

Discounts above 30% yield negative margins. Limiting promotions to a strict 25% ceiling protects the global portfolio's 23.65% avg profit margin from eroding into loss territory (as seen at 50% discount yielding -$62.04 per line).

2
Optimize Electronics Logistics Infrastructure

As Electronics constitutes 64% of total portfolio revenue ($3.38M), localized warehouse staging is critical. Improving supply chain latency for this single category acts as the primary anchor for absolute revenue growth.

3
Focus Marketing Spend on High-Margin Categories

Promote Clothing (24.80% margin) and Books & Media (23.61% margin) in targeted campaigns. Boosting volumes in these categories will organically drag the global average margin upward.

1
Enforce the Live ML Pricing Guardrail

Integrate the Gradient Boosting predictive engine into the merchant listing flow. Prevent sellers from creating listings with predicted margins below 10% based on discount-price coefficients.

Key Findings (Dataset 2)

Finding 1 · The Retention Crisis

Reviews Do Not Drive Loyalty

The platform's repeat purchase rate is a dismal 3.05%. Surprisingly, 5-star reviewers have a 3.31% return rate, while 1-star reviewers return at 3.22%. High customer satisfaction has almost zero correlation to loyalty.

Finding 2 · Seller Concentration

Top 17.5% Sellers = 80% Revenue

Only 543 of 3,095 active sellers generate 80% of all platform revenue. This Pareto concentration creates existential platform risk if top sellers leave.

Finding 3 · Revenue Driver

Installments Enable Larger Baskets

Customers buying in 1 installment spend ~$112 on average. Those using 10 installments spend ~$415 — a 271% increase in basket size driven purely by payment flexibility.

Finding 4 · Marketing Effectiveness

Merchandising Drives Sales Velocity

Targeted visual marketing matters: high-quality listings (7 photos) sell 4.37 units per listing compared to 2.62 for zero photos. Better digital merchandising directly correlates with peak sales volume and higher revenue.

Finding 5 · Temporal & Geo Density

SP/RJ/MG drives 62.5% Revenue

Purchasing is highly concentrated. The São Paulo, Rio de Janeiro, and Minas Gerais corridor drives over 62.5% of national revenue. Temporally, weekday revenue is 40% higher than weekends, peaking Mon-Wed between 14:00-22:00.

Strategic Recommendations (Dataset 2)

Empirical Action Items & Policies

1
Enforce Listing Quality Standards to Maximize Sales Velocity

Prompt sellers to optimize listing details by uploading 7 photos, utilizing 51–60 character titles, and writing detailed descriptions (2,000+ characters). Data shows this optimized listing structure increases sales velocity from a baseline of 2.62 to 4.37 units sold per listing.

2
Promote High-Installment Options Above $150 Baskets

Credit Card installments heavily inflate transaction values (10x plan averages $415 vs $112 for 1x). Make installment payments prominent on checkouts with values above $150.
